Craft outputs and weapons

Most crafts pair a durable main line with a consumable or enhancement sideline, so nearly every archetype reaches both permanent and consumable items (the one deliberate exception is called out below). Weapon-making is split four ways, so every weapon class has a maker and no single craft monopolizes weapons.

Inscriptiontomes, off-handsscrolls, glyphs, inks
Enchantingwands, staves, orbs and off-hand fociweapon and armor enchants

Bows, staves, and shields find homes among the ten: bows to Leatherworking (the bowyer-fletcher), shields to Armorcrafting, magical staves and wands to Enchanting, mechanical ranged (crossbows, firearms) to Engineering, metal melee to Weaponcrafting. Jewelcrafting is the nearest thing to a pure-permanent craft (its cut gems are reagents for other crafts rather than true consumables), but it only ever pairs with Enchanting or Weaponcrafting, both of which carry consumables. The Apothecary (Alchemy plus Cooking) is the deliberate mirror case: an all-consumable archetype with no permanent line of its own. Its permanent reach comes from its hobby (Enchanting or Jewelcrafting, up to the rare cap), and in exchange it is the game's consumables powerhouse.
