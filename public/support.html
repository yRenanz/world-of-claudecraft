Support

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Need help with World of ClaudeCraft? Contact support for account access, technical issues, gameplay problems, store app questions, bug reports, or data requests.

Contact us

Email support at the address below or contact us through the Discord server. Include your username, character name if relevant, the device or browser you are using, and a short description of the issue. Do not send your password.

Email woc@levystreet.com Contact us on Discord

What to include

  • Your World of ClaudeCraft username.
  • Your character name, if the issue is character-specific.
  • The platform you are using, such as iOS, Android, or web browser.
  • A clear description of what happened and what you expected.

Data deletion

For full account or data deletion requests, use the Data Deletion page or email support with your username and a clear request for account deletion.
