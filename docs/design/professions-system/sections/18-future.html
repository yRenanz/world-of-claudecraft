Future and esoteric skills

Two safe expansion lanes, neither of which touches the production wheel, and one committed but later-phase mechanic parked here until the core systems are proven.

A second, off-wheel system for non-combat crafts (if needed)

If the ten ever feel too limited, cosmetic and housing crafts can live in a separate, simpler system (a second wheel or a flat skill set) that feeds purely cosmetics and player housing and does not participate in the wheel's opposite/hobby structure. Candidates:

  • Carpenter (wood: furniture, housing frames, ships, instruments) and Mason (stone: walls, foundations, statues, runestones), the construction pair that did not fit cleanly on the combat wheel.
  • Housing-appropriate gathering and tending skills from the menu above, especially Shepherd, Beekeeping, and Stargazing.
  • Off-wheel cosmetic crafts that touch no combat power: Tattooing/Warpaint, Taxidermy, Heraldry/Banners, Instrument-making, Candlemaking.

Not built now. This lane exists so housing and cosmetics never pressure the combat wheel.

Sub-disciplines that fold under existing crafts

Poisoncraft and Perfumery under Alchemy; Glassblowing under Engineering or Jewelcrafting; Runecarving and Golemcraft under Enchanting; Bookbinding and Cartography under Inscription; Trapmaking under Engineering; Bonecarving under Jewelcrafting or Leatherworking. None of these is a new wheel axis.
