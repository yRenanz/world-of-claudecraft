Considered and discarded

Kept so settled ideas are not relitigated. Each was explored seriously before the current design won.

The discarded ideas and why
IdeaWhy discarded
Free 1-plus-1 hobby (any creative craft plus any tradition craft)Dilutes interdependence and becomes a solved min-max. Replaced by the capped, geometry-determined opposite hobby.
Two-layer model (a material radar plus a separate Experimentation-vs-Tradition slider)Two mechanics to learn and tune; the single wheel already carries that tension across its diameters, with complementary crafts opposite each other.
Collapse to eight crafts (Cooking into Alchemy, Engineering plus Jewelcrafting into Apparatus)Perfectly regular and all-good opposites, but loses Cooking and Jewelcrafting as distinct crafts and worsens the neighbor-lesser story. Kept the richer ten.
Grow to twelve with Carpenter plus Mason on the wheelWorks, but over-constrains the ring: two stretch archetypes, a full re-lay, and the mirror theorem makes the result hard to curate. Construction moved off-wheel.
Fixed domains (predefined master pairs)Replaced by the emergent any-adjacent-pair specialty, which gives more character variety for free.
Single-craft "bridge" specs (a lone Tinker or Lapidary)A lone master has only one opposite, so it cannot offer the two hobby candidates an adjacent pair does. Every craft masters as part of a pair instead.
Capped opposite-affinity carve-out (a second hobby on the opposite side)Interesting but an extra system; the single opposite-hobby already delivers the "people can be both" fantasy within the cap.
